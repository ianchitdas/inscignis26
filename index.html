About InSCIgnis

inSCIgnis is the annual mega science festival of Tezpur University. It is one of the leading and the biggest science festivals in North-East India, celebrated every year on the occasion of National Science Day, commemorating the contributions of the first Nobel Laureate in Physics for India, Sir C.V. Raman.
